Who We Are

Love INC is a partnership of Christian churches that have joined together to provide volunteer and financial support for the operation of Love INC, so that together we can help the poor in coordinated, tangible ways that demonstrate God’s love.

Our Vision and Promise

Our vision is to see Christian churches united in purpose and fully engaged in actively living out their faith by lovingly serving people in need in their communities.

Our promise is gratitude toward our volunteers and donors, hospitality to those in need, and integrity in all that we do.

Learn more about Love INC

Church Partnerships

Church partnerships are essential to the accomplishment of the mission of Love INC. Each church partner commits to joining with other churches to provide volunteer and financial support for the operation of Love INC. In return, Love INC works with churches wherever they are at, to encourage them and help equip them for service.

Love INC makes it possible for Churches of any size to effectively respond to the complex issues of need and poverty in their communities by connecting people in need with people who care.

Our Volunteers

Volunteering is what makes a community because it brings people together to work on a goal that matters. Whether it is a fundraiser, or to help a local family who has fallen in a time of calamity, volunteers make it happen. Community life is improved by aiding others and lending a helping hand.
